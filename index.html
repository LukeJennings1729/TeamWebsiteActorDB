About Me

Hello! My name is Ezster Kiss and I am a Physical Education major at SUNY New Paltz and I'm looking to make the world a better place!

I run a program for female athletes, teaching them all about issues that come up in women's sports. I also coach at a sports camp and do a lot of advocacy work for all students on the New Paltz campus.
